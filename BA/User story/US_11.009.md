**ID:** US_11.009 | Welcome Dashboard > User Authentication & Role

**Description:**

**As an** administrator,
**I want to** configure user authentication and role-based access control in Jenkins,
**So that I can** grant users different permissions (Admin, Developer, Viewer) and verify correct dashboard behavior through automated tests.

**Preconditions**

1. Jenkins instance is up.
2. User is logged in as **admin** (the default administrator account created during Jenkins installation).
3. The **admin** user has **Overall/Administer** permission.
4. No additional users have been created yet (except the default **admin**).
5. No jobs/items have been created in Jenkins (to see the Welcome Dashboard in its empty state).

**Acceptance Criteria:**

1. On the Main page of the Jenkins dashboard, I navigate to **Manage Jenkins** → **Plugins** → **Available**, search for **Role-based Authorization Strategy**, check the box next to the plugin, click **Install** and wait for installation to complete. Then I go to Manage Jenkins → Security → Security, go to the Authorization section, select **Role-Based Strategy** from the dropdown and click **Save** to enable role‑based access control in Jenkins.

2. I go to Manage Jenkins → Security. Clicking on **Manage and Assign Roles** (which appears in the Manage Jenkins menu after the plugin is installed and Role‑Based Strategy is saved) opens the role management page.


3. While on the **Manage Roles** tab, i can: create the following roles:
- In the **Global roles** section:
    - I enter **admin** in the **Role to add** field, click **Add**, and check the box in the `Administer` column for the `Overall` row (Overall/Administer permission).
    - I enter **role_developer** in the **Role to add** field, click **Add**, and check the following boxes in the **role_developer** column: `Overall/Read`, `Job/Create`, `Job/Discover`, `View/Create`, `View/Read`.
    - I enter **role_viewer** in the **Role to add** field, click **Add**, and check the `Overall/Read` box in the **role_viewer** column.

- In the **Item roles** section:
   - I enter **developer-project** in the **Role to add**, enter **.*** in the **Pattern** field, click **Add**, and check the following boxes in the **developer-project** column: `Job/Build`, `Job/Cancel`, `Job/Configure`, `Job/Read`, `Job/Workspace`, `Run/Delete`, `Run/Update`.
    - I enter **viewer-project** in the **Role to add** field, enter **.*** in the **Pattern** field, click **Add** and check the `Job/Read` box in the **viewer-project** column.
 I click **Save** at the bottom of the page.

4. I navigate to **Manage Jenkins** → **Security** → **Users**. I click the **Create User** button with a plus (+) icon and create:
  - `test_developer` with Username=`test_developer`, Password, Confirm password, Full name, E-mail address. I click button  **Create User**.
  - `test_viewer` with Username=`test_viewer`, Password, Confirm password, Full name, E-mail address. I I click button  **Create User**.

5. I navigate back to **Manage Jenkins** → **Manage and Assign Roles** and open the **Assign Roles** tab, where I assign the created roles to users:
  - In the **Global roles** section:

    - I find the existing user `admin` and then check the box for `admin` in the `admin` column.
    - I click **Add User**, type `test_developer` in the **User ID** popup window, click **OK**, and check the box for `test_developer` in the `role_developer` column.
     - I click **Add User**, type `test_viewer` in the **User ID** popup window, click **OK**, and check the box for `test_viewer` in the `role_viewer` column.

- In the **Item roles** section:
    -  I click **Add User**, type `test_developer` in the **User ID** popup window, click **OK**, and check the box for `test_developer` in the `developer-project` column.
     - I click **Add User**, type `test_viewer` in the **User ID** popup window, click **`OK`**, and check the box for `test_viewer` in the `viewer-project` column.



6. After I save the role assignments, I verify the correct behavior for each user:
  
 - I log out and sign in as the **Admin user** (username: admin with password). After logging in I am redirected to the **Main page** of the Jenkins dashboard. The left navigation shows the **New Item**,   the **Manage Jenkins** link is in the top-right, and the central Welcome panel displays the prompts: `Create a job`, `Set up an agent`, `Configure a cloud`, and `Learn more about distributed builds` (shown when no jobs exist).

- I log out and sign in as the **Developer user** (`test_developer` with password). I am redirected to the **Main page** of the Jenkins dashboard where **New Item** link is visible, **Manage Jenkins** link is NOT visible (hidden), and the Welcome Dashboard shows only `Create a job`.

- I log out and sign in as the **Viewer user** (`test_viewer` with password). I am redirected to the Main page of the Jenkins dashboard   where **New Item** link is NOT visible (hidden), **Manage Jenkins** link is NOT visible (hidden), and existing jobs are visible in read-only mode (no "Build Now" button, no configure option).
- I log out and attempt to log in with invalid credentials. I remain on the login page and see the error message: **Invalid username or password**.
